Frequently Asked Questions

How does the price tracker work?
The price tracker monitors flight and hotel prices in real-time and alerts you when prices drop or rise, helping you book at the best time.
What information do I need to provide to use the price tracker?
You need to enter your destination, travel dates, and optionally, your budget preferences.
How often does the price tracker update the prices?
Prices are updated frequently, typically every hour, to ensure you get the latest pricing data.
Does the price tracker cover all airlines and hotels?
Yes, our tracker includes most major airlines and hotels worldwide. However, some exclusive deals may not be covered.
Is there a fee for using the price tracker?
No, using the price tracker is completely free. We do not charge any fees for tracking prices.
Can I set up alerts for specific destinations or dates?
Yes, you can set up custom alerts for your preferred destinations and travel dates to receive notifications when prices change.
How accurate are the price predictions provided by the tracker?
Our price predictions are based on historical data and market trends. While they are generally accurate, prices can fluctuate due to unforeseen events.
What happens if the price drops after I book my flight or hotel?
Some airlines and hotels offer price protection policies. You may be able to rebook at the lower price or request a refund for the difference.
Can I track multiple destinations simultaneously?
Yes, you can track multiple destinations at the same time and receive alerts for each location separately.
Is my personal information safe when using the price tracker?
Yes, we prioritize your privacy and use secure encryption methods to protect your data. We do not share your information with third parties.
